About Us

The Georgia State Defense Force (GSDF) GEOINT Section is dedicated to providing geospatial intelligence (GEOINT) support for disaster response, emergency management, and operational planning. As a specialized unit within the GSDF, we leverage advanced mapping technologies, geographic information systems (GIS), and remote sensing to enhance situational awareness and decision-making for state and local agencies.
